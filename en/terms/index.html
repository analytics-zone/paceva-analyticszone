Terms and Conditions

Terms for using the Paceva website and local-only analytics application.

Last updated: June 2026

1. Use of Paceva

Paceva provides training analytics for personal use. You are responsible for the files you import, the permissions you grant, and the training decisions you make from the results.

2. No mandatory account

Paceva is designed to work without a mandatory account for local analysis. If optional export or sharing features are introduced, they must be explicit and separately controlled by the athlete.

3. Data ownership

Your workout files, biometrics, GPS tracks, and derived metrics remain yours. Paceva does not claim ownership over your training history.

4. Estimates and formulas

Metrics such as W' balance, CSS, critical speed, training load, HRV readiness, and gait efficiency are estimates based on available inputs and formula versions. Missing or unreliable sensor data can affect results.

5. Safety

Paceva is not a medical service or a substitute for professional coaching. Stop training and seek medical help if you experience pain, dizziness, chest symptoms, or unusual distress.

6. Contact

Questions about these terms can be sent to analyticszone+paceva@onmedic.org.
