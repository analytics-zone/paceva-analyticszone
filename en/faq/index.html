Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Paceva local-only multi-sport analytics, imports, metrics, exports, and privacy.

Is Paceva a coaching platform?

No. Paceva is the athlete-owned personal analytics app. Coach dashboards, comments, team management, and shared planning belong in a separate trainer product.

What files can I import?

The product direction covers standard endurance files such as FIT, GPX, and TCX, plus health data authorized by the athlete where supported by the platform.

What does local-only mean?

Workout files, samples, GPS tracks, and health context are parsed and analyzed on your device by default. There is no required account or automatic training-history upload.

Which sports are supported?

Paceva is designed for swim, bike, run, walking, hiking, cardio recovery, and health context. Triathlon is a strong use case, but not the only one.

Can I share data with a coach?

Only through explicit export or future scoped sharing that you control. Paceva should not expose your live training history to a coach by default.

Does Paceva replace medical advice?

No. Paceva provides training analytics and estimates. It is not a medical device, diagnosis tool, or emergency service.
