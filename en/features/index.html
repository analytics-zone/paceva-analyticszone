Product Features

Paceva focuses on local import, local calculation, clear confidence signals, and athlete-controlled export.

Local import

Bring in FIT, GPX, TCX, wearable workouts, and health context without making a cloud account the center of your training history.

Metric engine

Calculate W' balance, CSS, critical speed, training load, HRV readiness, gait signals, and sport-specific fatigue locally.

Data confidence

Flag missing power, noisy GPS, unreliable heart rate, pool-length mismatch, and estimated inputs before you trust a conclusion.

Unified, not generic

Paceva uses one athlete profile and one training-load model, but each sport keeps its own metrics and context. Swim efficiency is not treated like bike power, and hiking elevation load is not reduced to running pace.

Export on your terms

Backup packages and optional summaries should be explicit. Raw samples and GPS tracks should only be included when the athlete selects that scope.
