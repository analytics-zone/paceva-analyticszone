Who Paceva Is For

Paceva is for self-coached athletes who want serious analytics without handing their full training history to a third-party cloud.

Advanced amateurs

Runners, cyclists, swimmers, triathletes, hikers, and cross-training athletes who already use wearables and want better interpretation of their own files.

Privacy-sensitive athletes

Athletes who want to analyze GPS tracks, health signals, and workout samples without default cloud sync or trainer visibility.

Multi-sport planners

People balancing swim, bike, run, strength, walking, hiking, and recovery across a season rather than viewing each sport in isolation.

Not for team management

If the main need is assigning athletes, commenting on workouts, or managing a squad, that belongs in a coach-facing trainer product.
